The Mission

Our mission is to push the boundaries of the mobile frontier. Use Angular and NativeScript to create engaging mobile experiences across iOS, Android, and web.

However, we don't want you to build an app for the sake of it. We believe that software can make a real difference, so we challenge you to build something amazing that also makes the world a better place.

Collaborate with an NGO of your choice and work on a project that will help them Do Good. Don't worry if you don't have a project idea yet, we can help you find one.

Getting ready

You don't need to be an expert to compete. If you know Angular, you can pick up enough of NativeScript to complete the challenge.

Check out the Learning Resources page to find out how to learn everything you need to compete.
We will also run a few online sessions to show you how to get yourself started.

Connect

Join the NativeScript Community Slack, we've prepared a special slack channel #hacking-for-good, where you can connect with us and other participants.
